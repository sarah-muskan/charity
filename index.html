About Us

Care & Hope Foundation is dedicated to social welfare. We believe that compassion can change the world. By focusing on education and dignity for the elderly, we bridge the gap between need and opportunity.

Mission

To serve humanity through care, education, and compassion.

Vision

A society where no child is neglected and no elderly person is abandoned.

Our Programs

Orphan Care

Providing shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support to children in need.

Old Age Care

Medical assistance, safety, and social support for senior citizens to live with dignity.

Education

Helping underprivileged children access quality education to build a brighter future.
